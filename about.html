About Frame Of Boy

Welcome to Frame Of Boy, where every moment is captured with passion and creativity. Founded by Selvabharathi, Frame Of Boy is dedicated to preserving life's most precious moments through the art of photography.

Our Story

At Frame Of Boy, we believe in the power of storytelling through imagery. Every frame of the shutter is a moment frozen in time, encapsulating the emotions, experiences, and memories that define our lives. With a commitment to excellence and a keen eye for detail, we strive to capture the essence of each subject we photograph.

Meet the Photographer

Selvabharathi, the founder and lead photographer of Frame Of Boy, is a seasoned professional with a passion for visual storytelling. With years of experience in the industry, Selvabharathi brings a unique perspective and artistic flair to every shoot. Their dedication to their craft and ability to connect with clients on a personal level ensure that every frames is a true reflection of the moment it captures.

Our Approach

At Frame Of Boy, we believe in building meaningful connections with our clients. We take the time to understand your vision, preferences, and expectations, ensuring that every frames reflects your unique personality and style. Whether it's a wedding, portrait session, or special event, we approach each project with creativity, professionalism, and a personal touch.

Let's Connect

We're passionate about what we do, and we'd love to hear from you! Whether you're planning your dream wedding or looking to book a portrait session, we're here to help bring your vision to life. Contact us today to learn more about our services and schedule your consultation.
